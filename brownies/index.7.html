Brownies
Chewy Fudge Brownie Mix

Mix up a batch of these delicious, mouth-watering chewy fudge brownies and let the fudge ooze out of every bite!


Tweet

  • Directions
  • Nutrition Facts
  • Ingredients
  • High Altitude Baking

You Will Need

  • Fudgy Brownies:
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • Cake-like Brownies
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil

Pan Size / Bake Time

  • Metal Or Glass Pan: 13"x9": 21-23 minutes 9"x9": 28-31 minutes 8"x8": 30-33 minutes Makes 20 Brownies

Baking Instructions

  1. PREHEAT oven to 350°F for glass or metal pans, 325°F for dark or coated pans*. GREASE bottom of pan with shortening or cooking spray.
  2. EMPTY brownie mix, egg(s), oil and water in large bowl. STIR until well blended (about 50 strokes). SPREAD into greased pan and bake immediately.
  3. BAKE in center oven at 350°F following chart below. *ADD 3-5 minutes for dark or non-stick pans. Brownies are done when toothpick inserted 1 inch from edge of pan comes out clean. COOL completely in pan on wire rack before cutting and serving.
