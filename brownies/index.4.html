Top 10 List: Favorite Brownie Recipes

February 28, 2017  //  66 Comments »

A collection of the best brownie recipes on Brown Eyed Baker – plain, nutty, salty, frosted – favorite brownies for everyone!

[We continue to update the “Top 10″ series of posts with brownies!]

This is quite possibly one of my favorite top 10 lists… Brownies!! They are dense, fudgy, packed full of chocolate, and infinitely customizable depending on your favorite flavors. There are peanut butter brownies, salted caramel, cheesecake, frosted, frozen… you name it, and I guarantee a brownie has been made with it.

Below is an updated list of my 10 favorite brownie recipes… Enjoy!

MY OTHER RECIPES

Top 10 List: Best Brownie Recipes on BEB

1. Baked Brownie

This is “THE” Baked Brownie – the one that has been all the rage for years, hailing from the Baked cookbook, and for good reason – it bakes up perfectly dense and fudge-like with that glorious crackly top.

2. Better-Than-Box-Mix Brownies

This is my go-to recipe when I want a perfect, traditional brownie. It’s also the one I turn to as a base for creating some of those epic brownie recipes (see the buckeye brownies, below).

3. Buckeye Brownies

One of my all-time favorite brownie recipes, featuring the best flavor combo in the world – chocolate and peanut butter! I used my better-than-box-mix brownies, a buckeye-like filling, and topped it with a chocolate/peanut butter ganache. Heaven.

4. Chocolate Fudge Brownies with Chocolate Buttercream Frosting

Because sometimes you just need a little something MORE when it comes to brownies, so you add frosting (and sprinkles!).

5. Cream Cheese Brownies

A combination of my love for cheesecake and my love for chocolate are these amazing cream cheese brownies. Perfect little cream cheese swirls running throughout the brownies!

6. Frozen Brownie Sundae

I still remember making this the weekend that we moved four years ago, and everyone agreed that having it in the freezer was pretty much the best thing ever.

7. Marshmallow Crunch Brownie Bars

One of my first ventures in the world of crazy epic brownie concoctions, and still one of my favorites to this day. They get rave reviews wherever I take them.

8. Peanut Butter Cup Crunch Brownie Bars

This is the peanut butter cup version of the marshmallow brownies above, and if you’re a chocolate and peanut butter fanatic like me, you’ll go nuts over these!
