Brownie Brittle Ice Cream Sammies

These delicious treats are fun for the whole family! Try different flavors of Brownie Brittle, ice cream and nut butters to find your favorite.

Ingredients :
Large pieces of Chocolate Chip Brownie Brittle
Vanilla ice cream, softened (or desired flavor)
‪‎Peanut butter, smooth
Preparation
Spread peanut butter onto one piece of Brownie Brittle.
Scoop ‪‎ice cream‬ onto another piece of Brownie Brittle and gently press the two pieces of Brownie Brittle together to form a sandwich.
Freeze immediately, until ready to serve.
