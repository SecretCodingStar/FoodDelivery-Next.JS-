Slutty Brownie Recipe: Easy and Quite Sinful

November 18, 2013 by Kimberly Danger 2 Comments

This slutty brownie recipe is for serious chocoholics only. They’re called “slutty” because they’re easy… and quite sinful.  Who am I to argue with the name?   Serve with your favorite vanilla ice cream, warm- right out of the oven for a real treat.

Slutty Brownie Recipe

1 package chocolate chip cookie mix
1 box brownie mix
Eggs (according to package instructions)
Vegetable oil (according to package instructions)
1 package Oreo cookies

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare the cookie batter according to package instructions. Press on the bottom of a 9×13 pan lined with parchment paper. Meanwhile, make brownie batter according to instructions and set aside. Layer your Oreo cookies on top of the cookie layer. Pour the brownie batter on top and bake for 30-33 minutes.

Thanks to The Londoner for this decadent creation!
