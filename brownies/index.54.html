Brownie BOMB Bars!

Ingredients

    Bottom Layer
  • 1 Box Brownie Mix (follow box instruction fully and for cooking times.)
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/4 Vegetable Oil
  • 1/4 Water
    • Middle layer
  • 1 Jar (198 g) Jet-Puffed Marshmallow Crème
    • Top Layer
  • 2 Cups Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 3 Cups Rice Krispies

Instructions

MY OTHER RECIPES
  1. Spray a 9x13 pan with cooking spray.
  2. Bake Brownies as directed on box and let to fully cool.
  3. Spread Jet-Puffed Mallow Crème.
  4. Place Chocolate chips and peanut butter in a medium sized microwave bowl and microwave until well blended stopping at 1 minute intervals.
  5. Once melted stir in Rice Krispies and spread evenly over mallow crème.
  6. Cover and chill for two hours before cutting into squares
  7. Yields 24-36 bars depending on how big you like your squares
  8. ENJOY!
